Easy Split-Pea Soup

Description

A simple but hearty and nutritious split-pea soup. Intended for the Instant Pot, but works just as well on the stove.

This recipe eschews vegetables and a ham bone in favour of simplicity, allowing you to get a tasty bowl of soup to your table with little prep time and without having to have a ham dinner beforehand.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of split-peas
  • 1 - 450 gram (1 pound) ham steak
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 tbsp of chicken/vegetable boullion powder
  • 1 tbsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tsp of italian seasoning, or:
  • 1 tsp each of thyme and summer sage
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add split-peas, water, and spices to Instant Pot.
  2. Dice ham steak and add to pot.
  3. Close lid. Press pressure cook button. Cook for 25 minutes on high. (reduce by five minutes if you prefer more texture to your peas)
  4. Let naturally release for 10 minutes before quick release.
  5. Serve. Add salt and pepper to taste.
