Easy Chili

Description

Intended for the slow cooker but also great on the stove or in an Instant Pot, this easy chili leverages canned goods to keep prep time to a minimum.

This recipe serves as a great base to experiment with. Substituting the ground beef for another meat of your choice, mixing and matching or adding different kinds of beans, adding some vegetables, or throwing in some of your favourite hot sauce are just a few of the ways to spice up this simple dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450 grams) of lean ground beef
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 can of pork and molasses beans
  • 1 can of Aylmer Chili Seasonings Diced Stewed Tomatoes (if unavailable, use regular stewed tomatoes and season accordingly)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup of water (if cooking on stove or using Instant Pot to prevent burning)

Steps

  1. Add oil to pan. Set stove to medium-high. Brown ground beef, drain.
  2. Add ground beef, cans of beans, and can of tomatoes to slow cooker.
  3. Add the chili powder and red pepper flakes and any other spices you desire to slow cooker.
  4. Cook for 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low.
