Bean and Rice Stew

Description

A simple stew consisting of the classic staples beans and rice, with ground meat for added protein and spices for added flavour.

This recipe is intended for an Instant Pot, but works fine on the stovetop, with added time to reduce. As we are using black beans, no soaking time is necessary. Dried beans may be substituted for canned. If using canned, add the beans nearer to the end of cooking time. Less water or more reduction time may be needed in this case.

Ingredients

  • 280 grams of dried black beans
  • 360 grams of brown rice
  • 450 grams (1 pound) of ground meat of choice
  • 8 cups of water
  • 1 tbsp of cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp of chili powder
  • 1 tbsp of boullion powder (chicken/vegatable/beef)
  • 1 tbsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 2 tsp of red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Set instant pot to saute mode. Add oil. Brown meat, drain if necessary.
  2. Add tomato paste. Cook until brown deposits start to form on bottom of pot.
  3. Add some water and deglaze the pot. Add the rest of the water.
  4. Rinse beans and rice and add to pot.
  5. Add chili powder, boullion powder, onion powder, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes.
  6. Close lid of Instant Pot. Press pressure cook button and set to 20 minutes on high.
  7. Wait for cooking time to end and then for natural release.
  8. If there is too much liquid left in the pot, set to saute mode and reduce.
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste.
